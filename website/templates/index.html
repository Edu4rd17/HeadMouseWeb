Welcome to Head-Mouse Web!

Head-Mouse Web is an innovative web application that allows people with spinal cord injuries to navigate YouTube using only their head and eye movements. With our advanced computer vision technology, you can search for videos, play and pause them, adjust the volume and playback speed, and much more, all without using a traditional mouse and keyboard.
